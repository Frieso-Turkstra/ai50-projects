If I could live my dreams

By Frieso, 2022

The world closes its curtains
as I listen to my demons sing
a lullaby of solitude and fear
of the morning yet to come

My eyelids they grow heavy
I hear her voice from far away
swift she silences my demons
as I repose in her embrace

But then the morning comes
and she leaves before I wake
to the laughter of my demons
at my empty hands and heart

If only I could live my dreams
and the morning never comes
she would sing a song of silence
till her endless love is all I hear
